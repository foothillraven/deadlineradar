California CPA License Renewal Deadline by Birth Month

Wave 3 — California CPA license renewal

CPA license expires every 2 years at midnight on the last day of the licensee's own birth month. Licensees with an odd birth year renew in odd expiration years; licensees with an even birth year renew in even expiration years.
