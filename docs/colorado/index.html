Colorado CPA License Renewal Deadline

Colorado CPA license renewal

Certified Public Accountant (Individual Certificate)
Date not confirmed

Biennial, synchronized statewide (not cohort/anniversary-based). The Colorado Board of Accountancy (dpo.colorado.gov/Accountancy) states: 'All Certified Public Accountant (CPA) Certificates expire on November 30 of odd-numbered years.' Applies uniformly regardless of original issuance date, except new applicants issued within 120 days of the upcoming expiration are bumped to the following cycle's date. Renewal window opens approximately 4-5 weeks before the November 30 deadline. CPE reporting period runs January 1 of an even year through December 31 of the following odd year (80 hours total); the CPE completion due date (Dec 31 of the odd year) falls about a month after the certificate's own Nov 30 renewal date in that same year.

We could not confirm this date against two independent authoritative sources: the Board's own page states individual CPA certificates expire November 30 of odd-numbered years, but the governing rule (3 CCR 705-1, Rule 1.9(G)) and its underlying statutes (C.R.S. 12-100-107, 12-20-202) all delegate the exact renewal schedule to an unpublished schedule set by the Division of Professions and Occupations rather than stating November 30 themselves -- so this date remains single-sourced to the Board's own page. Confirm your exact renewal date with the Colorado Board of Accountancy (DORA) or on your license.

Public Accounting Firm Registration
Date not confirmed

Triennial, synchronized fixed calendar date board-wide (not firm-anniversary based). The same official Board page states 'All Public Accounting Firms (FRM) Registrations expire on August 31, every three years' -- but does not name which specific years anchor the cycle.

Independently re-verified: the dedicated firm-applications subpage and the Code of Colorado Regulations PDF both returned HTTP 403 on every fetch attempt (bot-blocked, not confirmed dead); no official Colorado source found states the specific anchor year. Do not compute or display a specific firm date until the anchor year is confirmed directly with DORA (303-894-7800).
