Hawaii CPA License Renewal Deadline 2027

Hawaii CPA license renewal

Verified
Individual CPA license/permit and firm permit
December 31, 2027

Biennial. The CPA license (HRS § 466-5(j)) and the individual and firm permits to practice (HRS § 466-7) all expire December 31 of every odd-numbered year -- the same fixed date across all three credential types, no cohort split.
