Illinois CPA License Renewal Deadline 2027

Illinois CPA license renewal

Individual CPA license expires September 30, every 3 years. Current cycle ends 2027-09-30.

Source of record 68 Ill. Admin. Code 1420.80(a) read the rule →
Firm license
Date not confirmed

Firm licenses expire November 30, on a 3-year cycle (68 Ill. Admin. Code 1420.80(b)) -- the same rule section that governs individual renewals, just a different subsection. The rule codifies the recurring month/day and the 3-year interval, but does not itself pin a specific anchor year for the firm track.

We do not have a codified anchor year for the firm track (unlike the individual track, which has a confirmed current cycle ending 2027-09-30). A documented 2021 IDFPR variance extended that cycle's firm-license expiration from November 30, 2021 to January 31, 2022 -- but it's a non-codified administrative order, not rule text, so we don't treat it as a codified second source; we're not projecting a current cycle from it. Confirm your firm's exact renewal date with the Illinois Dept. of Financial and Professional Regulation or on your permit.
