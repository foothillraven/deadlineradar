Wisconsin CPA License Renewal Deadline

Wisconsin CPA license renewal

Individual CPA License
Date not confirmed

Biennial. Wisconsin DSPS sets a single statutory renewal date for all individual CPA licenses: December 14 of each odd-numbered year (compliance biennium runs December 15 of an odd year through December 14 of the next odd year) -- fixed calendar, not birth-month or cohort-based; even a newly-licensed CPA still renews on this same date rather than getting an extended first cycle. CPE compliance period is the 2-year period immediately preceding renewal (80 credit hours, min 40 formal learning, 3 ethics, at least 20 hours in each 12-month half); no separate CE-reporting deadline apart from the Dec 14 renewal date.

We could not confirm this date against two independent authoritative sources during our 2026-07-05 audit: the Board's own page cites Wis. Stat. 440.08(2) and says December 14, but the actual statute text says December 15 -- a one-day conflict between a source and its own cited authority. Confirm your exact renewal date with the Wisconsin DSPS Accounting Examining Board or on your license.

Accounting Firm License
Date not confirmed

Same statutory date as individual licenses: December 14 of each odd-numbered year, biennial. No separate firm-specific cycle.

We could not confirm this date against two independent authoritative sources during our 2026-07-05 audit: the Board's own page cites Wis. Stat. 440.08(2) and says December 14, but the actual statute text (the firm-specific subdivision, 440.08(2)(a)3) says December 15 -- the same one-day board-vs-statute conflict found on the individual-license record. Confirm your exact renewal date with the Wisconsin DSPS Accounting Examining Board or on your license.
